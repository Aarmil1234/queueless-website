Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. What is a Digital Queue Management System?

A Digital Queue Management System is software that helps hospitals and clinics manage patient flow efficiently. It organizes appointments, generates queues automatically, and reduces waiting times for patients.

2. How does a queue management system work?

A queue management system organizes patients automatically based on appointments or arrival time, ensuring smooth patient flow. It provides real-time updates to both staff and patients, reducing wait times and overcrowding.

3. How does an online booking system work?

An online booking system allows patients to schedule, reschedule, or cancel appointments through a web or mobile platform. It syncs automatically with the hospital’s schedule, ensuring efficient patient flow and reduced waiting times.

4. How is HMS used for appointment scheduling?

A Hospital Management System (HMS) schedules patient appointments by allowing staff and patients to book, reschedule, or cancel slots digitally. It automatically updates the doctor’s calendar and optimizes patient flow to reduce waiting times.

5. What are the benefits of the hospital management system?

A Hospital Management System improves patient care, reduces waiting times, streamlines hospital operations, and enhances staff efficiency and decision-making.
